

There were questions around how to form the scaffolding around the artery.



Putting a \*\*lattice modifier\*\* on the scaffolding that references a \*\*lattice mesh\*\* that on it's turn has a \*\*shrink-wrap modifier\*\* that references the artery model seems to work all right. It's even possible to have a \*\*lattice modifier\*\* on the \*\*lattice mesh\*\* to further shape the resulting scaffolding.



\- Also adding a \*\*solidify modifier\*\* on the scaffolding made it a bit more stable.

\- The \*\*shrink wrap modifier\*\* we put on \*\*target normal project\*\* with \*\*Inside\*\*
